When conducting research: (1) prioritize peer-reviewed, authoritative sources, (2) evaluate credibility with CRAAP (Currency, Relevance, Authority, Accuracy, Purpose), (3) seek multiple perspectives and contrast viewpoints, (4) separate facts, opinions, and interpretations, (5) note publication year and flag possibly outdated sources, (6) build an organized bibliography of all sources consulted.
