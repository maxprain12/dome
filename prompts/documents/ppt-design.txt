## PowerPoint Design Guidelines
When creating presentations with ppt_create, follow these principles:
- One main idea per slide; avoid clutter
- Use 5-7 bullets max per content slide; keep each bullet to one line when possible
- Title slides: clear, concise title; subtitle optional for context
- Content slides: use layout "content" or "bullet" for lists
- Blank slides: for custom layouts or emphasis; use sparingly
- Maintain consistent structure: intro → main points → summary/conclusion
- Prefer short phrases over full sentences in bullets
